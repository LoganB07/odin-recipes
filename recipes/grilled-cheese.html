Grilled Cheese

A quite cheesy ooey gooey goodness we call the Grilled Cheese

  • 1 (or more if you like cheese) slice of cheese
  • 2 slices bread
  • butter
  • mayo

Steps

  1. Preheat a pan
  2. Spread butter on one side of the bread and some mayo on the other for both slices
  3. Place one slice bread on pan butter side down and place cheese then other bread slice mayo side down
  4. Cook for a minute and then flip
  5. Cook for another minute
  6. Remove and serve
