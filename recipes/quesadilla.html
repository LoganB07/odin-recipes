Quesadilla

A yummy and delicous quesa-ma-dilla that you can make for yourself or share with others!

  • 2 Tortillas
  • 1 fuckton Shredded Cheese
  • Chipotle Sauce (Skip this ingredient if your name is Meghan)

Steps

  1. Preheat a pan on the stove
  2. Apply a genorous amount of chipotle sauce to 1 Tortilla and press both tortillas together
  3. Seperate tortillas and place 1 onto the pan sauce side up
  4. Spread a fuckton of cheese onto the tortilla
  5. After a few seconds place the other tortilla on top sauce side down
  6. Flip after a minute
  7. Cook for another minute and then remove the quesadilla
  8. Slice and serve
